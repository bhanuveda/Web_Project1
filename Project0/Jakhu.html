Jakhu Temple

About Jakhu Temple :

Jakhu Temple is an ancient temple in Shimla, dedicated to the Hindu deity Hanuman. It is situated on Jakhu Hill, Shimla's highest peak, 2.5 km (1.6 mi) east of the Ridge at a height of 2,455 m (8,054 ft) above sea level. Each year, a festival is held on  Dussehra , before 1972 the festival was used to held at Annadale .

Temple Information:

  • Darshan Timings: 6:30 am to 7:30 pm

    • Note: Timings may vary on special occasions and on festivals

  • Festivals Celebrated:
    1. Dussehra(Vijayadashami)
    2. Hanuman Jayanthi
  • Ropeway Details:
